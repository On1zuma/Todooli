Notice and Consent

Igonii provides clear and transparent notice to our users about the types of personal data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with. We obtain explicit consent from our users for the collection and processing of their personal data.

Data Processing and Security

Igonii implements strict security measures to protect our users' personal data from unauthorized access or breaches. We regularly review and update these measures to ensure that they remain effective.

Data Access and Portability

Igonii provides our users with the ability to access, correct, and delete their personal data. Additionally, we allow users to request a copy of their personal data in a machine-readable format.

Data Breaches

Igonii has implemented procedures to detect, investigate, and report data breaches to the appropriate authorities. We will notify affected users without undue delay if a data breach occurs.

Data Protection Officer

Igonii has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO) who is responsible for monitoring compliance with GDPR and providing advice and guidance on data protection matters.

International Data Transfer

Igonii ensures that any transfer of personal data outside the EU is done in compliance with GDPR.
